# UNPRO Market Intelligence Agent

The Market Intelligence Agent analyzes demand signals to identify market opportunities.

## Responsibilities

- Detect high demand services
- Identify underserved clusters
- Identify services lacking professionals
- Detect seasonal opportunities

## Data Sources

- `property_projects`
- `market_demand_signals`
- SEO analytics
- `service_seasonality`

## Outputs

- Recruitment targets
- Service opportunity reports
- Cluster growth recommendations

## Execution Frequency

- **Weekly**: Analysis of demand signals
- **Monthly**: Strategic recommendations
